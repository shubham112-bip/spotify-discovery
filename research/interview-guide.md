# Interview Guide — Commute Listeners (30 min)

**Goal:** Validate AI review insights with primary research.  
**Do not lead** with AI or solution ideas until the final question.

---

## Before the call

- [ ] Send calendar invite + Meet/Zoom link
- [ ] Ask permission to record: _"Is it OK if I record for note-taking? It won't be shared publicly."_
- [ ] Open `interview_notes_template.md` for this participant

---

## Script

### Warm-up (3 min)

- "Thanks for joining. There are no wrong answers — I'm interested in your real habits."
- "Walk me through yesterday — when did you listen to Spotify, and what did you play?"

### Commute / routine ritual (7 min)

- "Think about your typical commute [or chore routine]. How do you decide what to play *before* you start?"
- "Do you ever open Spotify and scroll without picking anything? What happens?"
- "What's on repeat this week — playlists, albums, or artists?"

### Repeat behavior (7 min)

- "Tell me about the last time you played the same playlist 5+ days in a row. What was going on?"
- "What would have to be true for you to *not* use that playlist?"
- "Is repeating music a problem for you, or does it feel fine?"

### Discovery attempts (7 min)

- "When was the last time you tried to find something *new* on Spotify? Walk me through it."
- "How did it end — did you save anything, or go back to familiar music?"
- "What felt risky or annoying about trying something new in that moment?"

### Spotify surfaces (5 min)

- "Discover Weekly — useful, useless, or you forget it exists?"
- "Daily Mix, Radio, Spotify DJ — anything you trust? Anything you ignore?"
- "If a recommendation is wrong in the first 30 seconds, do you skip, or give it a chance?"

### Ideal + close (3 min)

- "If Spotify understood your commute mood tomorrow morning, what would it do differently?"
- "Anything I didn't ask that matters for how you listen?"

## After the call (within 30 min)

Fill in `interview_notes_template.md`:
